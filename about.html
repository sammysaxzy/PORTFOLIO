About Me

Get to know more about my journey and passion

"Data is the new oil, and I'm here to refine it into valuable insights."

Hello! I'm Makinde Samuel

I'm an aspiring Data Analyst and Data Scientist with a passion for extracting meaningful insights from complex datasets. My journey in data analytics began with a curiosity about how data can transform businesses and drive informed decision-making.

Currently, I'm undergoing intensive training in Data Analytics at 8thGear (LSETF Sponsored) and Data Science/Machine Learning at 3MTT/DeepTech (FG Sponsored). These programs have equipped me with cutting-edge skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, and machine learning frameworks.

Through my internships at Elevvo and FibreHub, I've gained hands-on experience in data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, dashboard creation, and presenting data-driven insights to stakeholders. I've worked on real-world projects involving survey data analysis, retail analytics, and business intelligence reporting.

Beyond data analytics, I bring practical technical experience from my role at Sparktek Communications, where I've installed and maintained network infrastructure, CCTV systems, and provided technical support to clients. This background gives me a unique perspective on how technology and data intersect in solving real-world problems.
